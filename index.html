About Us

Hi, I’m Ismaila Jallow 🕊️

I’m a Gambian man, born and raised in Jalangbam. As a boy growing up here, I always wished we had a cozy place to sit, work, and enjoy a really good cup of coffee.

In 2023, I decided to stop wishing and start building. I opened Baobab Bites Café with my own savings, one coffee machine, and so much hope. I wanted to create a space where students, families, and friends in Jalangbam could feel proud, welcome, and caffeinated.

For me, coffee is everything. It’s strength, comfort, and community in a cup. That’s why I created our Baobab Latte — my favorite. It’s espresso and steamed milk with a little taste of home: baobab powder. It’s become what we’re known for.

Our team is at the heart of Baobab Bites Café. My employees are warm, hardworking people who help keep every cup perfect, every pastry fresh, and every guest smiling. They greet customers, prepare drinks with care, and make sure every booking feels welcome.

Our customers keep me going. They say things like “Ismaila’s coffee is the best in town” and “this place feels like family.” As a man running this business, hearing that from my own community means the everything to me.

Baobab Bites Café is more than coffee. It’s a dream come true. Thank you for being part of it.

With love, — Ismaila
